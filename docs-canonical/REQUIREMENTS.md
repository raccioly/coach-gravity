## User Scenarios

### User Story 1 - First-Time Learner Onboarding (Priority: P1)

A non-programmer receives the Coach Gravity ZIP, opens it in Antigravity, and types `/start`. The AI agent guides them through trust-building (Phase 0), building their first app (Phase 1), and understanding what happened (Phase 2).

**Acceptance Scenarios**:
1. A learner with zero coding experience types `/start` and receives Phase 0 onboarding content from the AI agent
2. A learner in Phase 1 follows the AI's step-by-step guidance and has a working web application at the end of the phase

### User Story 2 - Reference Lookup During Building (Priority: P2)

A learner in the middle of Phase 5 (building their project) needs to look up a concept or troubleshoot an error.

**Acceptance Scenarios**:
1. A learner building their project references the glossary and finds clear definitions of all technical terms used in the curriculum
2. A learner encountering an error checks the troubleshooting guide and finds actionable steps to resolve common issues
