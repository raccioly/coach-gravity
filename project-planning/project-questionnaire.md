# 📋 Project Questionnaire

> Answer these questions before building your project. Don't overthink — just write what comes to mind. You can always change things later.
>
> **How to use:** Fill in each answer below, then share this with Antigravity when you start building. Or, just type `/start` and it will ask you these questions conversationally.

---

## 🎯 Project Vision

**1. What is this project?** *(Describe it in 1-2 sentences as if you're explaining to a friend)*


**2. Who is this for?** *(Clients, customers, your team, the public?)*


**3. What problem does it solve?** *(What's the pain point or need?)*


**4. What does success look like?** *(How will you know this project is "done" or working?)*


---

## 🏗️ Scope & Features

**5. What are the must-have features?** *(List the things it absolutely needs to do)*


**6. What are the nice-to-have features?** *(Things that would be great but aren't critical)*


**7. Are there existing tools or services it needs to connect to?** *(Email, payments, databases, APIs, etc.)*


**8. How many pages or screens will it need?** *(Just a rough estimate)*


---

## 👤 Users & Access

**9. Who can access it?** *(Public — anyone? Private — login required?)*


**10. Are there different user roles?** *(e.g., admin vs. regular user vs. viewer)*


**11. How will people log in?** *(Email/password? Google? Magic link? No login needed?)*


---

## 🎨 Look & Feel

**12. What's the vibe?** *(Professional? Fun? Minimal? Premium? Playful? Dark? Light?)*


**13. Any websites you admire?** *(Share URLs of designs you like)*


**14. Do you have a logo or brand colors?** *(If yes, describe or attach)*


**15. Light mode, dark mode, or both?**


---

## 📱 Platform

**16. Where will people use it?** *(Desktop? Mobile? Both?)*


**17. Any specific browser requirements?** *(Usually "no" is fine)*


---

## 🔧 Technical

**18. Do you have a domain name?** *(e.g., myapp.com — if not, do you want one?)*


**19. Where should it be hosted?** *(If unsure, say "AWS" — that's our standard)*


**20. Do you already have any data?** *(Spreadsheets, databases, CSV files, etc.)*


**21. Do you need to store user data?** *(Names, emails, preferences, uploaded files, etc.)*


---

## 📊 Business Context

**22. Is this for a business or personal use?**


**23. Will you charge users?** *(Subscription? One-time purchase? Free?)*


**24. What's your timeline?** *(Any deadlines?)*


**25. What's your budget for hosting/services?** *(Free tier is fine for most prototypes)*


---

## 💡 Anything Else?

**26. Is there anything else we should know?** *(Industry regulations, accessibility needs, competition, inspiration, etc.)*
